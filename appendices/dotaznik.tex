\section*{Introduction}
\textbf{A quick guide to IEQ and IAQ}

Before you start, please take a moment to understand two key terms in this survey:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.6em]
\item IEQ (Indoor Environmental Quality) is the broad term for the overall quality of a building's environment. It covers all factors that affect a person's health and comfort indoors, including thermal comfort (temperature), lighting, and acoustics (noise), as well as air quality.
\item IAQ (Indoor Air Quality) is a crucial component of IEQ that focuses specifically on the air you breathe. It measures pollutants like carbon dioxide (CO2), particulate matter (e.g., PM2.5), and humidity levels.
\end{enumerate}

In short: IAQ is about the air, while IEQ is about the total indoor experience.
